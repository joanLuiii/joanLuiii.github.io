About Me

Hi, nice to meet you!

I'm Joan a UX/UI Designer in Singapore.I enjoy collaborating with people from different background, and solving complex problems together.

Before this I was a software engineer in a healthcare project, where I worked on backend programming, developing and fixing reported problems/bugs of the system.

I spoke to my users on several occasions. Only after communicating with the users did I realise that instead of allowing ease of use, the complex design of the system actually did the very opposite – it hindered my users into committing more human errors, causing potential risks to the patients’ safety.

Thus hatched my desire to learn how to design better interface to create meaningful and better experiences for my users.
